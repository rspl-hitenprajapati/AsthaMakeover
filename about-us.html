About Astha

Beauty with Purpose. Training with Impact.

Astha Makeover and Academy is a modern beauty studio in Nadiad, Gujarat, founded to help women feel confident and build professional opportunities in beauty.

Founder & Journey

Owner: Asmita Prajapati

Established: 2023

Location: Nadiad, Gujarat

Starting as a passion-driven initiative, the salon quickly gained trust for bridal artistry, skin care precision, and warm client service. The grand opening phase led into milestone growth through 2025 with strong local word-of-mouth.

Mission

To empower every woman with confidence through expert beauty services and professional training.

Vision

To become a trusted destination in Gujarat for premium makeover services and career-ready beauty education.

Core Expertise

  • Bridal and party makeup with HD/Airbrush options
  • Hair styling, coloring, smoothing, and treatment care
  • Advanced facial and skin correction services
  • Nail extensions, nail art, waxing, threading, mehndi

Academy Programs

  • Makeup courses (Basic to Advanced)
  • Hair cutting and styling programs
  • Skin treatment learning modules
  • Nail art and extension training
  • Certification provided after course completion

Team

A growing team of trained beauticians and makeup artists committed to consistent quality, hygiene, and personalized care.

What Makes Us Different

Astha blends service excellence and skill development in one space, helping clients glow confidently and students grow professionally.
